How to Play

you're a gifted high school chemist who was diagnosed with cancer, what else were you supposed to do? you've become the best cook for thousands of miles, which is why your employer, local fast-food chain owner Gus Fring, has such a tight grip on you. search around your lab for everything you need to deliver your usual product to Gus, or even something extra special. when you think you have found the right chemicals, press "start cooking" to begin your process.
